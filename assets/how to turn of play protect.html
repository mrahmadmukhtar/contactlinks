How to Turn Off Play Protect

Follow these steps to turn off Play Protect:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device.
  2. Tap the three horizontal lines in the top-left corner to open the menu.
  3. Select "Play Protect."
  4. Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to access settings.
  5. Toggle off the "Scan apps with Play Protect" option.
